Badminton Clash 3D

Badminton Clash 3D is a thrilling casual 1v1 sports mobile game. With amazing physics, easy controls and stunning 3D graphics, you'll have an immersive and fun gameplay experience. From the cheering crowd to the swish of the racket, every detail is designed to make you feel like you're in the game with a chance to score at every turn.

The goal was to design a game that captures the feeling of playing badminton in real life.
Designed the gameplay from ground up with 2 developers and a producer. We succesfully delivered a final prototype, that was approved by stakeholders for the production phase.

Key contributions included:
  • Gameplay design and mechanics.
  • Mathematical equations that govern the physics.
  • In-game UI/UX design.
  • Design documentation.
  • Placeholder assets.
  • Real-life badminton research.
  • Market analysis.
