Pizza Craft

The easy and fun way to make delicious pizzas for your customers.

Pizza Craft is a casual fun game that I made for UltraMelon Apps. As the sole developer on the team my responsibility was to program the game from scratch in Unity. The game was primarily developed for the iOS platform.

How to play:
  • Tap & drag to place the ingredients.
  • Speed Increases the farther you get!
  • Use all 5 ingredients to make what the customers want.
  • Craft like a pro and get the highest score!
Features:
  • Conveyor gets faster the more pizzas you make
  • Do or Die game play (Only one mistake allowed)
  • 5 yummy ingredients to use to make a pizza!
