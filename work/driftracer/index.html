Drift Racer

Drift Racer is an adrenaline-pumping online multiplayer racing game where players master high-speed drifts on challenging tracks. With smooth controls and intense visuals, it offers a competitive and immersive experience for racing enthusiasts.

As the lead designer oversaw the game design team's work and contributed to:

- Tuning gameplay for an optimal racing experience.
- Designing two race tracks and unique car profiles.
- Creating cinematic sequences using Cinemachine.
- Designing UI/UX and multiplayer user flow.
