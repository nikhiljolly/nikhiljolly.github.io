Dead Dreams

Play


A game about an ordinary boy, with an extraordinary childhood, marred with stressful memories. Will he be able to survive another nightmare?

Dead Dreams is a game that I made with Rahul Parihar in 2 days for the BYOG 17 game jam. In the game you play as a boy who is harrassed by dark memories and has to fight back with the few good memories that he reminisces.

The game was built from scratch in Unity3d. I primarily focused on development, while Rahul focused on making the 3d art and models. We both worked together in the ideation and game design process. After a lot of dabbling around with lights and post-processing effects, we finalized the low-poly pixel art style that you can currently see in the game.

Guide:
Press left click to call good memories towards you, and release to shoot.
Designed with using Bootstrap.
